Does the AI actually process refunds, or just suggest?+
Both — you choose. On Level 1 it only suggests. On Levels 2–4 it acts automatically within your parameters. Every automated action is logged and reversible within 24 hours.
Do I need a developer to set this up?+
Not at all. Enter your Shopify URL, authorize the connection, done. Under 2 minutes, zero technical knowledge. No App Store, no code, no developer calls.
What data does RefundOs access from my store?+
Read access to orders, customers, and products — plus write access to issue refunds. We never access payment details. Revoke access instantly from your Shopify admin.
What if the AI makes a wrong decision?+
Every automated action is fully logged and reversible within 24 hours. Fraud-likely requests always default to manual review.
Does this work for stores outside the US?+
Yes. Any Shopify store, any country. Shipping estimates are localized by carrier data, and currency calculations use live exchange rates.
Can I cancel anytime?+
Yes. Cancel in one click from your dashboard — no calls, no retention flows, no minimum terms.
